Founded 2013

A Studio Built Around the Idea of Slow.

We started in a small Brooklyn loft with one belief — that beautifully made clothing should feel like a quiet luxury, not a loud statement.

A decade later, we still design every piece in-house, work with the same trusted American mills, and obsess over the details our customers tell us they notice — the weight of a sweater, the drape of a trouser, the way a coat sits across the shoulders.

We believe in fewer, better things. We believe that what you wear every day deserves the same care as something you wear once. And we believe in showing up for our customers — every order, every season.
